Ceviche (Peruvian Style)

Description

A trip to the gastronimic powerhouse of Peru is not complete without trying the region's specialty: ceviche. Readers are encouraged to seek out the most hidden restaurant serving only construction workers and elder locals, a surefire authenticity guarantee and one-way-ticket to experience the most flavorful fish your bland American palatte has ever come to imagine.

Ingredients

  • Fresh-caught sole, squid, shrimp, or seabass
  • Cancha Salada (fried Peruvian corn nuts)
  • Lettuce
  • Red Onion
  • Sweet Potato

For the Leche de Tigre:

  • Ice
  • Celery
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Aji Limo
  • Red Onion
  • Cilantro
  • White fish and/or shrimp

Steps

  1. Put all Leche de Tigre ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth.
  2. Strain Leche de Tigre, discarding the solids, and set aside.
  3. Cut and marinate the fish in the Leche del Tigre.
  4. Peel and steam the sweet potato.
  5. Serve marinated fish with lettuce, spoon over the leche de tigre and garnish with the cancha, julienned red onion, and sweet potato.
